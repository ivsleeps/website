About us

Hi, my name is Ivy and my path to tattooing was a little different. Art was always the only thing that gave me purpose and something I always came back to, after all I've been drawing since I could hold a pencil. Still, I've spent years in other industries as life seems to know better what one needs at any given moment. Tattooing came to me at the perfect time and since then I've been doing what I love most - I get to create, and together with you we create something lasting, endless, and that image when your eyes light up when you look at your new part and you're satisfied is priceless to me. The most important thing for me is that people feel comfortable and know that any type of person can find a safe space with me. I understand that sometimes arranging the whole process can be harder for someone, so I always adjust my approach based on the client and try to create a space for everyone..
