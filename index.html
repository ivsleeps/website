About me

Hi, my name is Ivy and my path to tattooing was a little different. Art was always the only thing that gave me purpose and something I always came back to — I've been drawing since I could hold a pencil. I've spent years in other industries as life unfolded, and tattooing arrived at the perfect time. Since then I've been doing what I love most: creating work that lasts, and collaborating with clients to make pieces that feel right.

The most important thing for me is that people feel comfortable and know that any type of person can find a safe space here. I adjust my approach to each client so the process is approachable and respectful for everyone.
